# Greeting Style

Use a restrained, professional first-contact tone for recruiter messages.

## Tone

- professional
- concise
- calm
- specific

Default tone for this user:

- `专业克制`

## Must Do

- point to one concrete need in the JD
- connect that need to one real experience point
- keep the message readable in one short screen

## Must Avoid

- praise-heavy openings
- generic statements about passion or learning ability
- repeated JD keywords with no evidence
- long self-introductions
- obvious AI wording

## Pattern

Write in three short moves:

1. acknowledge the role focus
2. connect with one relevant experience or capability
3. ask naturally to continue the conversation

## Example Shape

```text
您好，看了下这个岗位，和我过往做数据中台/指标体系相关产品的经历比较贴近。我之前做过这类平台型需求抽象和落地推进，对数仓、指标口径和跨团队协同这块比较熟。如果您这边方便，我想进一步了解下这个岗位当前最核心的建设重点。
```

Treat the example as shape guidance, not a reusable template.
